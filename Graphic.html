Course Description


This course introduces learners to the world of graphic design, teaching essential design principles, tools (like Canva, Adobe Photoshop, or Illustrator), and hands-on skills to create logos, posters, social media graphics, and more. Ideal for beginners looking to build a creative career or enhance their digital presence..



What you will learn

  • Understand graphic design principles
  • Work with top design tools (Canva, Photoshop, Illustrator)
  • Design social media posts, flyers, logos, and posters
  • Manage design projects from idea to final export
  • Build a portfolio of your creative work


Course Outline



Week 1: Introduction to Graphic Design

  • What is Graphic Design?
  • Career opportunities and applications
  • Understanding different types of design (print, digital, branding)
  • Overview of design tools: Canva, Photoshop, Illustrator

  • Color theory and psychology
  • Font types and font pairing
  • Layout principles (alignment, contrast, white space)

Week 3: Working with Canva (No-Code Design Tool)

  • Getting started with Canva (Free version)
  • Using templates, elements, and images
  • Designing Instagram posts, flyers, YouTube thumbnails
  • Exporting designs for print and digital


Week 4: Adobe Photoshop Basics

  • Introduction to Photoshop interface and tools.
  • Working with layers, text, shapes, and brushes
  • Editing and enhancing photos
  • Hands-on: Create a promotional banner


Week 5: Logo Design and Branding

  • Principles of logo design
  • Color, shape, and typography in logos
  • Creating simple logos in Canva or Illustrator
  • Hands-on: Design your own brand logo


Week 6: Poster & Flyer Design

  • Elements of a good poster/flyer
  • Visual hierarchy and call-to-action
  • Designing an event poster (Photoshop or Canva)
  • Assignment 1: Create a poster for a real or fictional event


Week 7: Business Card & Brochure Design

  • Standard dimensions and bleed settings
  • Designing a professional business card
  • Creating bi-fold or tri-fold brochures
  • File export for print (PDF with bleed and crop marks)


Week 8: Social Media Graphics for Marketing

  • Branding for Instagram, Facebook, YouTube
  • Designing story templates, ad creatives, thumbnails
  • Scheduling and organizing designs using content calendars
  • Quiz 1: Design principles and Canva usage


Week 9: Adobe Illustrator Basics (Vector Design)

  • Introduction to vector graphics
  • Using Pen Tool, Shape Builder, and typography
  • Creating flat icons, custom illustrations
  • Hands-on: Design a vector badge


Week 10: Portfolio Development & Case Study

  • Collecting and organizing your best work
  • Creating mockups to present your designs
  • Writing design case studies
  • Quiz 2: Photoshop & Illustrator knowledge check


Week 11: Client Work & Freelancing

  • Understanding client briefs and revisions
  • Creating moodboards and proposals
  • Platforms to find freelance work (Fiverr, Upwork, Behance)
  • Hands-on: Simulated client project


Week 12: Final Project & Presentation

  • Final Project: Design a full brand kit (logo, social posts, flyer)
  • Presentation: Explain your design choices
  • Assignment 2: Write a reflection on your graphic design journey
  • Feedback and career roadmap discussion
