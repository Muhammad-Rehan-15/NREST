Course Description


This course teaches you how to build and manage an e-commerce store using Shopify, one of the most powerful platforms for launching online businesses. Youâ€™ll learn how to create a fully functional store, manage products, set up payments, and customize your design without needing advanced coding skills.



What you will learn

  • Set up and configure a Shopify store
  • Add and manage products and collections
  • Customize your store design using themes
  • Configure payment gateways and shipping
  • Manage orders and customers
  • Optimize your store for sales and marketing


Course Outline



Week 1: Introduction to Shopify and E-Commerce

  • What is E-Commerce and its types (B2B, B2C, etc.)
  • What is Shopify and why use it
  • Overview of e-commerce business models (Dropshipping, POD, etc.)
  • Creating a Shopify account (Free Trial)
  • Shopify Dashboard walkthrough
  • Store settings overview


Week 2: Store Setup and Branding

  • Choosing and customizing a store name
  • Connecting custom domain (if available)
  • Adding store logo, favicon, and branding
  • Selecting currency, timezone, and language settings

Week 3: Product Management

  • Adding single and bulk products.
  • Managing product descriptions, pricing, and images.
  • Using product variants (sizes, colors)
  • Creating collections (manual & automated)
  • Inventory management tips


Week 4: Theme Customization and Store Design

  • Exploring free and paid Shopify themes.
  • Installing and customizing themes
  • Editing homepage, header, footer, and menu
  • Adding banners, slideshows, and featured products


Week 5: Pages, Menus, and Navigation

  • Creating About Us, Contact Us, FAQ, Privacy Policy pages.
  • Building store navigation menu (main menu & footer menu).
  • Linking collections and pages to menus
  • Quiz 1: Test your understanding of store structure


Week 6: Payment Gateways and Shipping

  • Setting up payment gateways (Shopify Payments, PayPal, etc.).
  • Setting tax and shipping zones.
  • Free shipping and flat-rate shipping setup.
  • Checkout customization


Week 7: Order Management and Customer Handling

  • Understanding the order flow.
  • Fulfilling orders and adding tracking info
  • Managing customer profiles and communication
  • Handling returns and refunds


Week 8: Shopify Apps and Tools

  • Introduction to Shopify App Store.
  • Essential apps: Reviews, SEO, Email, Upsell, etc..
  • Installing apps and managing permissions.
  • Using apps for dropshipping (like Oberlo or DSers)


Week 9: SEO and Store Optimization

  • Adding meta titles and descriptions.
  • Using image ALT text and friendly URLs.
  • Creating blog posts for traffic.
  • Speed optimization techniques


Week 10: Marketing and Social Media Integration

  • Connecting Facebook, Instagram, and Google.
  • Using Shopify Email Marketing.
  • Setting up discount codes and coupons.
  • Quiz 2: Marketing and SEO basics


Week 11: Launching the Store

  • Testing your store before launch.
  • Going live with a custom domain.
  • Payment & checkout test transactions.
  • Legal pages and policies review


Week 12: Final Project and Assessment

  • Final Project: Launch a full demo store.
  • Students present their Shopify stores.
  • Assignment: Marketing strategy plan.
  • Feedback session and next steps (Shopify Plus, dropshipping, etc.)
